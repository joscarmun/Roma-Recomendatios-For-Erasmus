What we think about our Erasmus in Rome

“Although Erasmus lasts only one year, you'll remember it for a lifetime — especially when you live in a city that gives meaning to all of our history. Living in Rome is a unique opportunity to discover art, history, and experiences no other place in the world can offer. While Erasmus life is full of plans that can distract you from the city you're in, I think it's essential to truly live and explore Rome — not treat it as just a background to your Erasmus. At first, you may doubt leaving your family, friends, university, and hometown... but don’t overthink it. Step out of your comfort zone, and you'll discover a new version of yourself — and a new reality.”

– Jorge
